## ❓ FAQ Section

1. **Do I need to pay per user?** No. Every plan covers your whole team up to the limit. No per-seat charges.
2. **Can I change plans later?** Yes. Upgrade any time. Downgrade at end of billing period. No contracts.
3. **Does Ledge do billing and invoicing?** You create and track invoices in Ledge — they sync to QuickBooks automatically.
4. **What if I only need some modules?** That's the point. You decide what to turn on. You're never paying for routing and dispatch you'll never use.
5. **Who is Ledge built for?** Small contractors $500K–$5M revenue, 3–15 employees. Landscapers, hardscape, fencing, concrete, any trade with a project lifecycle.
